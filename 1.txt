Lua regex does not have alterations, lookarounds and optional capture groups! 
NO | for sequence alternation. (Character sets [...] are for single character alternation).
NO lookarounds.
NO non-capturing groups (?:...). All () are capturing.
? applies to the immediately preceding item (character, class, NOT capture group) to make that item match 0 or 1 times.
